What is it with all these dots?

The dot stands for .hiv - the domain-ending for the end of AIDS

The domain ending .hiv incorporates the fight against AIDS into our everyday digital lives. Every .hiv web address raises funds and awareness for the end of AIDS. Every click on a .hiv domain triggers a micro-donation for selected HIV projects.
